[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the thunder rolling right beneath your skin
The kind of midnight magic where the wild begins
You pull me closer, letting all the rain pour down
She loves like a storm, tearing up this town

[Verse 1]
There's a fire in the shadows of this candlelit room
And your eyes are glowing brighter than the harvest moon
We've been talking for hours, but your touch says more
Leaving all our heavy secrets right beside the door

[Pre-Chorus]
I can feel the temperature starting to rise
Every time I get lost in the dark of your eyes
It's a slow-burn pulling me into the deep
A beautiful danger I want to keep

[Chorus]
Oh, you crash into my heart, lightning in the dark
Leaving every other memory just a single spark
I surrender to the rhythm, keeping me so warm
'Cause heaven only knows, she loves like a storm

[Post-Chorus]
